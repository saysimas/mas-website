UNTITLED MAS WAM STORIES PROJECT

In our first unit of the year, students were asked about the stories that connect them to the city of San Antonio. Each student wrote, produced, and edited their own visual retelling of a short story important to them. Click any of the pins to hear a story from one of our WAM students!
